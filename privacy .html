Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone, school affiliation, age/grade
  • Progress data: course completion, quiz scores, time spent
  • Payment data: processed via Razorpay; we do not store card/UPI details
  • AI mentor chat logs, for improving mentor responses and safety monitoring

2. How we use it

To provide course access, track progress, issue certificates, personalize AI mentor guidance, and communicate with students/schools about their enrollment.

3. Children's data

For users under 18, we collect only what's necessary for the course and require parental/school consent at signup. We do not sell any student data.

4. Sharing

We share data only with: Razorpay (payments), Firebase/Google Cloud (hosting/infrastructure), and the enrolling school (progress/attendance dashboards). We do not sell data to third parties or advertisers.

5. Security

Data is stored via Firebase with access-controlled security rules restricting each student's data to their own account.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting support@waves4youth.in.

7. Retention

We retain student data for the duration of enrollment plus a reasonable period afterward for certificate verification purposes.
